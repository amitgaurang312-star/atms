About Us

Advanced Care,
Compassionate Healing

Amritasha Hospital & Trauma Center is a premier healthcare facility comprising a 50-bedded hospital spread over a campus of 28,000 sq. ft., with 5 floors and a basement parking area — each floor having 12,000 sq. ft.

We are equipped with the latest technology for trauma care and all services for resuscitation and preservation of life. Our OPD complex features reception, consultation rooms, OT complex with C-Arm facility, post-op recovery area, and diagnostic services. Our ICU provides Ventilator Support for critical patients. Additional amenities include a canteen, laundry, and 24/7 ambulance services.

Advanced blood banking services — 24 hours, 7 days a week

Why Choose Our Blood Center?

Our state-of-the-art Blood Center is equipped with advanced technology for comprehensive blood banking, donation, and therapeutic services, ensuring safe and timely blood supply for all patients.

Blood grouping, typing & cross matching (Gel Technology)
Irregular Antibody Tests (ICT & DCT)
Rh Antibody Titre & Screening Tests (Hep B/C, HIV, Syphilis, Malaria)
Whole Blood, Packed RBC, Saline Washed Packed Red Cells
Leukoreduced Red Cells & Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)
Cryoprecipitate & Random Donor / Single Donor Platelets
Thalassemia & Sickle Cell Anaemia Care
Pedi-packs for Newborns & Pediatric Care
Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)
Therapeutic Plasma Exchange & Phlebotomy
Red Cell Exchange
